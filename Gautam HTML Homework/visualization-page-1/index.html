City Latitude vs. Max Temperature
In this visualization, which compares the latitude of the cities and the maximum temperatures experienced by those cities, we can observe that the closer the city is to the equator(0 degrees latitude) the higher the temperature spikes. Something else that can be noticed is that during the earlier months of the year, the southern hemisphere tends to be warmer than the northern hemisphere. This could be due to the axis tilt of the Earth. Furthermore, there are a few outliers whose temperatures stand out. An assumption can be made that these cities are outliers due to their geographical positioning on the planet.
